Office of Admissions

The Office of Admissions of Don Honorio Ventura Technological State University provides for the selection of prospective students regardless of age, race, gender, religion, nationality and economic status. It defines the policies and practices of admissions, which are reflective of the objectives of the institution.

Qualified applicants who will be entitled to profit from the educational programs of the College are admitted to the University. The following factors are considered in the evaluation of application for admission: past scholastic records, scholastic aptitude, character, recommendations and applicant's acceptance of the policies, rules and regulations of the College and of the University.

Privacy Policy Agreement

The Don Honorio Ventura State University is collecting personal information for the purpose of determining your eligibility for admission, facilitating Don Honorio Ventura State University Admission Test(DHVSUAT), releasing DHVSUAT results, consideration for scholarship and enrollment.

When registered, all the information collected will become part of your permanent record that will be kept for good by the University. The results of your DHVSUAT are valid for one year of school. Your personal information will be kept by the University for one academic year for those who applied but did not proceed with the registration and for those who did not qualify. After that, all documents will be disposed.

We Ensure that your informations will kept private and only used for this admission for following RA 10173, or the Data Privacy Act of 2012.
